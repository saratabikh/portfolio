Safety in Monash High Powered Rocketry

Safety Lead • March 2024 – Present

Role

Safety Lead

Team Size

15-person safety team, 150+ members supported

Recognition

Jim Furfaro Award for Technical Excellence

Focus

Risk Assessments, SWIs, event risk management

Scope

As Safety Lead at Monash High Powered Rocketry, I manage a dedicated safety team and support the development of a team-wide safety culture. My work centres on standardising safety documentation, integrating safety gates into design reviews, and enabling safe testing at launches and outreach events.

Process Improvements

Standardised Risk Assessments and Safe Work Instructions

Revamped legacy safety procedures by creating standardised Risk Assessments (RAs) and Safe Work Instructions (SWIs) to improve consistency across the team.

Safety Gates in Design Reviews

Introduced safety gates in design reviews so hazard control is considered early in the engineering process, supporting a proactive approach across 150+ members.

Event Risk Management

Organised logistics for testing, outreach, and launch events by developing event risk management plans and overseeing team execution to safeguard all attendees.

Safety at High-Risk Propulsion Tests

Alongside propulsion responsibilities, I provided safety representation for high-risk systems such as high-voltage spark ignitors. I developed detailed risk assessments and safe work instructions in collaboration with designers and academic advisors.

Impact

These safety improvements supported safe testing of advanced propulsion systems and contributed to the team receiving the Jim Furfaro Award for Technical Excellence among 140+ student teams.
